Keyboard Shortcuts

ShortcutAction
⌘ Cmd + ZUndo
⇧ Shift (tap, any selection mode)Toggle current mode's sub-mode: Relational ↔ Discrete
⇧ Shift + click toolbar selection buttonActivate mode in Discrete sub-mode (default is Relational)
⇧ Shift + drag (edge mode, edges selected)Extrude selected edges
⇧ Shift + drag (any selection mode, no edges selected)Rubber-band marquee selection
